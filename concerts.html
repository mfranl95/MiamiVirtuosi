First Fridays at
First Miami

MIAMI VIRTUOSI is proud to announce a new concert series on Brickell Avenue called First Fridays at First Miami. The concerts will take place on the first Friday of each month at 8:00 PM at the historic, strikingly beautiful, and acoustically superb FIRST MIAMI PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH located at 609 Brickell Avenue. The concerts will feature artists who live, trained or have marinated in Miami for a significant season of their lives.
