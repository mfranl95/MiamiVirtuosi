About Miami Virtuosi

The mission of MIAMI VIRTUOSI is to enrich the cultural vitality of Miami through music. The name Miami Virtuosi evokes its altruistic core value: to support virtue in our great and influential city. Miami Virtuosi CELEBRATES VIRTUOSITY by featuring Miami artists in CONCERTS, IMPACTS MIAMI CULTURE by providing top level professional musicians for WEDDINGS and EVENTS and RAISES UP THE NEXT GENERATION OF VIRTUOSI through private and group music LESSONS.

Ross Debardelaben

Founder
